# Where Winds Meet - Game Context

## Overview

**Where Winds Meet** is an epic open-world action-adventure RPG rooted in the rich legacy of Wuxia. Set during the turbulent era of tenth-century China, players take on the role of a young sword master, uncovering forgotten truths and the mysteries of their own identity. As the wind stirs across mountains and rivers, so too will their legend rise.

## Setting

- **Time Period**: Five Dynasties and Ten Kingdoms (10th century China)
- **Location**: Ancient China - from bustling imperial capitals to hidden wilderness
- **Genre**: Wuxia (martial arts fiction) + Open-world Action RPG

## Key Features

### An Era on the Brink. A Hero on the Rise.
Immerse yourself in the captivating Five Dynasties and Ten Kingdoms period of China, where epic battles and legendary tales come to life. From the bustling imperial capital to the hidden wilderness, every path is filled with secrets and adventures. Freedom carries weight—stir up mischief or take the noble route: befriend villagers, forge alliances, and carve your legacy as a Wuxia hero.

### An Open World of Infinite Possibilities
Explore a vibrant world filled with life, from bustling cities to hidden temples in lush forests—all dynamically shaped by time, weather, and player choices. Experience Wuxia-style traversal: scale rooftops, glide through the air, or use fast travel between regions. Uncover thousands of points of interest across 20+ unique regions.

### Master Your Way of Wuxia Combat
Tailor combat approach—melee, ranged, or stealth. Engage in combat with classic Wuxia weapons: swords, spears, dual blades, rope dart, bows, fans, and umbrellas. Switch weapons and employ martial arts and mystic skills like Tai Chi to outmaneuver enemies.

### Adventure Alone or Forge Your Community
Embark on a narrative-driven adventure filled with rich solo gameplay, or team up with up to four friends for seamless co-op experiences. Join or create a Guild to access group activities—intense guild wars, multiplayer dungeons, and epic raids.

## Core Themes

### Martial Arts (Wuxia)
- Martial arts schools and sects (门派)
- Internal energy cultivation (内功/Neigong)
- Qi (气) - life force energy
- Master-student relationships
- Legendary weapons and techniques

### Chinese Culture
- Poetry, calligraphy, music
- Tea ceremonies and social rituals
- Buddhist and Taoist philosophy
- Traditional festivals and customs

### Political Intrigue
- Conflicts between kingdoms
- Court politics and espionage
- Loyalty and betrayal

## Tone Guidelines

- **Formal dialogue**: Polite forms, court language
- **Combat descriptions**: Dynamic, impactful
- **Lore/History**: Respectful, slightly archaic
- **Common NPCs**: Natural, period-appropriate
- **Item descriptions**: Evocative, culturally authentic
